Privacy Policy

Gmail Attachment Downloader

Last Updated: May 30, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Gmail Attachment Downloader ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our service at https://hardiksondagar.github.io/gmail-attachment-downloader/.

Information Collection and Use

The Gmail Attachment Downloader only requests read-only access to your Gmail account through the Gmail API. We specifically use the scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly

This allows our app to:

  • Read and search your emails
  • Access attachment metadata and content
  • View your email address and profile information

How We Use Your Information

We use the access granted to:

  1. Search your emails based on criteria you provide
  2. Display emails with attachments
  3. Download attachments upon your request
  4. Generate ZIP files of multiple attachments
  5. Export email content as JSON when requested

Data Storage

The Gmail Attachment Downloader:

  • Does NOT store your emails or attachments on any server
  • Does NOT transmit your data to any third parties
  • Processes all data entirely within your browser
  • Temporarily stores authentication tokens in your browser's localStorage (encrypted) for session persistence
  • Does NOT track your usage or behavior beyond what's needed for the app to function

Data Retention

  • Email content and attachments are only stored in your browser's memory during your active session
  • Authentication tokens are removed when you sign out
  • No persistent data is kept after you close the application

Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following third-party services:

  • Google Gmail API for email access
  • Google Identity Services for authentication

No other third-party services are used for data processing or analytics.

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • Client-side only processing to prevent data exposure
  • Encrypted local storage of authentication tokens

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to the application at any time through Google Account Permissions
  • Request information about your data usage (though no data is stored externally)
  • Report any concerns about this privacy policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
